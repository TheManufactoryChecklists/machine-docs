## ⚠️ Critical Safety Rules
- **Never operate without required training** (if applicable).  
- **Stay alert** — no distractions, no loose clothing, no gloves near rotating tools.  
- **Inspect the machine before use** — damage, missing guards, loose parts.  
- **Report issues immediately** to staff or via the maintenance form.  
- **Know the emergency stop** location and test it if appropriate.

---

## ✔️ Pre‑Use Checklist (30–60 seconds)
- Machine is clean and free of debris.  
- Guards, shields, and safety devices are in place.  
- Workpiece is appropriate for this machine.  
- Bits/blades/cutters are sharp and properly installed.  
- Dust collection or ventilation is connected (if required).  
- Power switch is OFF before plugging in or adjusting.

---

## ▶️ Basic Operating Steps
1. Set up the workpiece and secure it properly.  
2. Adjust fences, stops, or guides as needed.  
3. Set speed/feed/power settings appropriate for the material.  
4. Stand in a safe position (describe if specific).  
5. Turn the machine ON and let it reach full speed.  
6. Perform the operation with controlled, steady movement.  
7. Turn machine OFF and wait for full stop before removing material.

---

## ❌ Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Using the wrong bit/blade/material.  
- Forcing the cut or feeding too fast.  
- Reaching across moving parts.  
- Leaving the machine running unattended.  
- Using the machine while tired or distracted.

---

## 🧹 Post‑Use Responsibilities
- Power OFF and wait for full stop.  
- Clean the machine and surrounding area.  
- Return jigs, bits, blades, and accessories to their storage locations.  
- Empty dust collection (if required).  
- Log any issues or damage.
